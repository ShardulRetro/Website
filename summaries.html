Understanding the costs associated with your education is essential. Here is an overview of tuition and fees:

  • Tuition: The cost of instruction and academic services.
  • Fees: Additional charges for facilities, technology, and other services.
  • Total Cost: The combined amount of tuition and fees for the academic term.

We offer flexible payment options to make managing your expenses more convenient:

  • Full Payment: Pay the entire amount at the beginning of the term.
  • Installment Plans: Break down payments into manageable installments.
  • Financial Aid: Explore available financial aid programs to offset costs.

Our refund policy ensures fairness in financial transactions. Here are key points to note:

  • Refund eligibility criteria, such as withdrawal deadlines.
  • Process for requesting and receiving refunds.
  • Timeline for processing refunds.

Access your financial statements for a detailed breakdown of transactions and balances:

  • Billing Statements: Monthly or term-based statements outlining charges and payments.
  • Transaction History: Track all financial transactions related to your education.
